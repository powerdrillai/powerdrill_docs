## Step 1: Sign in to Powerdrill

To start your Powerdrill journey, first you need to sign in to Powerdrill.

1. Visit https://powerdrill.ai/ and click **Sign In** in the upper-right corner.
2. In the **Sign In to Powerdrill** dialog box, select a sign-in method and click **Sign In**.
    You can sign in with your Google or GitHub account if you have one. 
    
    Alternatively, you can enter your email address to sign in. After you enter your email address and click **Sign In**, an confirmation email will be sent to you. You need to log in to the given email and confirm the sign-in.

## Step 2: Import your data

After sign-in, you can create your datasets and import your data. If you want only public information that can be obtained from the Internet, you can jump to [Step 3](#step-3-extract-intelligence-from-your-data). However, we recommend that you create your datasets to have better experience of certain Powerdrill features that mainly differentiate Powerdrill from general chatboxes.

1. On the Powerdrill console, choose **Datasets** from the left-side bar and click the **+ Dataset** button on the upper-right corner.
2. On the **Create Dataset** page, specify the dataset name and description and click **Continue**.
    You can choose whether to select the **Accessible as a ChatGPT Plugin** option. If selected, the dataset will be available on third-party platforms.
3. Associate the dataset with the Apps that you want to allow access to data sources in the dataset.
4. Choose a data source type and configure the data source.
    Powedrill supports various data source types, including plain text, web pages, and files in PDF, CSV, JSON, Text, PowerPoint, Word, or Excel format.
5. Configure the data source and click **Finish**.

:::info
You can skip steps 4 and 5 to create an empty dataset and choose to add data sources later.
:::


## Step 3: Extract intelligence from your data

After configuring the dataset, you can start use the associated App to start extract intelligence from your data.

:::info
If you skip [Step 2](#step-2-import-your-data), you can still use the Apps to chat based on information from public networks.
:::

1. Open the App.
    Method 1: On the **Datasets** page, click the App name in the **Associated Apps** column of the dataset.
    Method 2: On the left-side bar, choose **Apps**. On the **Apps** page that appears, select the desired App.
2. Start your chat.
