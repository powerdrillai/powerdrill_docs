## Overview

[Powerdrill](https://powerdrill.ai/) is built for bridging your data and AI. It provides the services and platform for no-code and one-stop integration of your data and OpenAI large language models (LLMs) for intelligent Q&As and ecosystem interaction.

Powerdrill is currently free for trial. After sign-in, you'll get some message credits and dataset storage size for free. For more information, see the [Pricing](https://powerdrill.ai/pricing) page.

## Highlights

### Build your AI knowledge base over your data

Powerdrill streamlines the creation of AI LLM-powered knowledge bases over your data, with ZERO line of code. All you need to do are:

1. Create a dataset with data sources imported. Data sources can be text, URLs, or files of various types.
2. Connect your dataset to OpenAI.

Now, you can chat with OpenAI.


### Surpass ChatGPT accuracy

For questions on your datasets, a Powerdrill App can yield more accurate answers than ChatGPT.

### Versatile OpenAI ecosystem support


Powerdrill supports many features to enable you to access OpenAI ecosystem tools:


- ChatGPT client access without an OpenAI account
- ChatGPT interaction with datasets without an OpenAI account
